# Closeness centrality 

## Use-cases-whento use the Closeness Centrality algorithm 

- Closeness centrality í used to research organizational networks, where individuals iwth high closeness centrality are un a favourable position to control acquire vital information and resources within the org.

- Closeness centrality can be interpreted as an estimated time of arrival of information flowing through telecommunications or package delivery networks where information flows through shortest paths to a predefined target. It can also be used in networks where information flows through shortest paths to a predefined target. It can also be used in networks where information spreads through all shortest paths simultaneously, such as infection spreading through a social network..

- Closeness centrality has been used to estimate the importance of words in a document, based keyphrase extraction process.
